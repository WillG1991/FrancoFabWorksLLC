About Me

About Me

Learn more about Louis Franco, the person behind Franco Fabworks LLC

Hello there! I'm Louis Franco, the proud owner of Franco Fabworks LLC. I've called Brick my home for the past decade, and for the last 6 years, I've been a homeowner in this wonderful community. My journey with welding and fabrication began while working as a welder at a major chemical plant. Over the years, my passion for this craft grew, leading me to establish Franco Fabworks. If you ever find yourself in need of general repairs or custom fabrication services, particularly with aluminum or steel, don't hesitate to reach out to us.
